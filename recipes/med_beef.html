Mediterranean Beef Rice

Home

Description

Get transported to Greece with this spin on the Asian staple.

Flavourful mix of beef and fried rice with a refreshing Greek Salad topping.

Ingredients

  • 1 lemon
  • 2 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 cups cooked long-grain rice (cold leftovers are best)
  • 1 tsp. ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp. ground coriander
  • 1 pt. grape or cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 seedless cucumber, cut into 1/4" pieces
  • 2 Scallions, Sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint, roughly chopped
  • Crumbled feta cheese, for serving

Directions

  1. Finely grate the zest of the lemon, then cut the lemon in half. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the beef and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned, 4 to 5 minutes (spoon off and discard any excess fat). Add the garlic and 1⁄4 teaspoon each salt and pepper and cook, stirring, for 1 minute; toss with the lemon zest. Transfer the beef to a bowl and squeeze the juice of half a lemon on top.
  2. Wash and dry the skillet, then heat the remaining tablespoon oil over medium-high heat. Add the rice and season with the cumin, coriander, and 1⁄4 teaspoon each salt and pepper. Cook, tossing occasionally, until the rice begins to crisp at the edges, about 5 minutes. Fold in the beef mixture and remove from heat.
  3. Meanwhile, squeeze the juice of the remaining lemon half into a medium bowl. Toss with the tomatoes, cucumber, scallions, and 1⁄4 teaspoon each salt and pepper. Fold in the mint and serve over the rice. Top with crumbled feta, if desired.
